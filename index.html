What is WorkTonic?

Work Tonic is a division of Drawing Board (www.drawing-board.in) a boutique management consulting firm since 2017), that enrolls unemployed graduates and post graduates who are keen to find employment in Sales, Marketing, Customer Care, Secretarial roles, HR, Operations. Work Tonic nourishes them with classroom based coaching, imparting necessary functional & higher order soft skills and find them competent employment which would reward them personally and professionally for their career growth.

What kind of placements are likely to be offered for students?

Work Tonic is a finishing school and provides required hard skill and soft skill training and prepare them for a job interview. Work Tonic trains the students in acquiring necessary functional knowledge, higher order soft skills and preparation for interviews such as CV preparation / mock Group Discussions and mock interviews. All these inputs are very useful in the selection process.

Work Tonic collaborates with the reputed and growing companies to offer placement assistance for its candidates. Companies across Industries such as Telecom, Real Estate, FMCG, Durables, Retail, Food and Beverage, Fashion are in discussion with Work Tonic for hiring. All companies are excited as Work Tonic proposes to provide candidates who are functional basics ready, come with right attitude and have good value system.
